## Slide 2: The Problem
*   Farmers lack motivation to adopt sustainable practices.
*   Traditional methods are resource-intensive (water, fertilizer).
*   Lack of real-time, personalized guidance.

## Slide 3: The Solution - EcoFarming
*   **Gamification**: Turn farming tasks into "Missions".
*   **AI-Powered**: Personalized advice based on crop, weather, and season.
*   **Community**: Connect farmers, share success, compete on leaderboards.

## Slide 4: Key Features
*   **Daily Missions**: "Inspect for pests", "Apply organic manure".
*   **EcoScore**: A credit score for sustainability.
*   **Rewards**: Badges, streaks, and panchayat-level recognition.
*   **Multilingual**: Accessible in Hindi, Marathi, English.
